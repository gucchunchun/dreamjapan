about us

there are many Japanese in abroad with big energy and dreams.

but there is no opportunity to show their dreams to anybody.

to support their dreams, we made this DREAM JAPAN

we connect those dreams and show you Japanese passion and dreams through this festival.

Our logo symbolizes the unity and collaboration of individuals, each with their unique dreams.The holding hands emphasizes the spirit of teamwork and cooperation. Through our distinctive logo, we express our commitment to achieving meaningful dreams and aspirations.
